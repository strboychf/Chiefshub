About Me

Hey, I’m Dickson Chief, but online you probably know me as @strboychf. I’m a 16-year-old content creator with a unique background — born in Nairobi, Kenya, and proud to represent both my Kenyan and Australian heritage. Growing up, I was always drawn to storytelling, expression, and the power of creating something that connects with people all over the world.

I started my content journey on TikTok, where I built a community through humor, creativity, and raw, relatable moments. That passion quickly spread to YouTube and Instagram, where I continue to push boundaries and showcase what it means to be original in a digital world full of trends. Whether I’m dropping a skit, a behind-the-scenes moment, or something totally unexpected, it’s always 100% me.

What drives me? Freedom. Vision. Legacy. I’m not just creating content — I’m building a world. My world. A space where people feel entertained, inspired, and seen. That’s why my motto is:
“I'm a god in my own world.”

So if you’re into fearless creativity, real talk, and unapologetic vibes — welcome home. This is just the beginning.
